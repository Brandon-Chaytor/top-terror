Why so scary?

Late September 2023 I began my first project in Get Coding. Being a horror film enthusiast, and an indecisive individual, TopTerror was conceived! The creation of this website was intended to help ease the burden of picking and keeping track of good horror films. The horror genre is rich with content both new and old. Navigating this hellscape of potential dumpster-fire content however, is probably the biggest challenge of any horror fan. TopTerror aims to address this challenge and slash it open once and for all!
